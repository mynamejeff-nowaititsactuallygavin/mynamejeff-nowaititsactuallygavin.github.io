This method is not very common but is fun if you are bored of methods such as Sipping or "Strawing." As the name suggests, The "Four Legged Mammal" method involves getting down on your knees, and licking up the water with your tongue, I've seen some people lift their arms off the ground while doing this, requiring them to use muscles in their stomach/hip area to keep their head over the water. Most four legged mammals, like cats and dogs, have specially shaped mouths so they can lift mouthfuls of water with ease, Humans do not have this kind of tongue, so it takes training to teach your tongue how to cup the water, and lift it into your mouth.
